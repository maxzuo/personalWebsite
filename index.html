About Me

I am currently a senior at Lincoln Sudbury Regional High School in Massachusetts. I am involved in several different aspects of school life. For example, I have been the president of the Programming Club for a year now, and as president I have coordinated my school participation of several hackathons. I myself was the first place winner of the MIT Blueprint 2017 hackathon. I also play the piano and violin, and I was a soloist in the orchestra I participate in at Rivers School. I also have been awarded in an international piano competition the opportunity to perform at Carnegie Hall in New York City. I am also a Peer/Student Leader, helping to make my school a warm and welcoming place for everyone. I speak at several school-wide assemblies, and personally welcome the incoming 9th graders each year. Additionally, as a Peer Mediator, I help to resolve conflicts between students without the need of punishments.
